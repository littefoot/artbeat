The Artist

ROMEL

Romel is an artist whose work functions as a living archive. Shaped by travel and lived experience, his practice creates images that exist between structure and intuition.

With a deep connection to his Caribbean roots, the Dominican Republic became the point of return—a place that defined Artbeat as a quiet search for home, where the colours and textures of its people revealed the weight of what art can hold.

The Process

Each piece of art begins as a real-world story. An image captured in the moment—on the streets of Europe or the coasts of the Caribbean—then reimagined.

Working across photography, painting, and digital intervention, Romel acts as an observer using visual notes. These are not just aesthetic decisions; they mirror how memory forms. Incomplete, textured, and shaped by attention. The image is held long enough to be felt, allowing form and light to carry meaning without explanation.

Philosophy

Bold without breaking the vow of care.

In a world of noise, Artbeat offers a pause. It is not about trend or spectacle, but about noticing.

We value the balance between discipline and intuition. Romel stands behind the work not to define its meaning, but to protect the space where it exists. As collectors, we do not just acquire an image; we become part of an ongoing record—a shared responsibility to honour the story it tells.

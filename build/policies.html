Effective Date: Jan. 23, 2026

Thank you for your purchase. We take pride in the quality of our artwork, printing, and framing. Please read our refund policy carefully before placing an order.

1. Custom & Made-to-Order Items

All custom prints, custom-framed artwork, made-to-order products, and personalized items are final sale and non-refundable once production has begun.

This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Custom-size prints
  • Custom framing or matting
  • Limited editions or signed artworks
  • Products printed or framed specifically for the customer

2. Non-Custom / Ready-Made Items

Non-custom, ready-made items may be eligible for a return within 7 days of delivery, provided that:

  • The item is unused and in original condition
  • The item is returned in original packaging
  • The customer contacts us before returning the item

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item is defective or incorrect.

If your order arrives damaged, defective, or incorrect, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with:

We are not responsible for delays caused by shipping carriers, customs, weather conditions, or events beyond our control. Shipping fees are non-refundable once an order has been shipped.
